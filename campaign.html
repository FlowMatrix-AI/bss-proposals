Optimization Rules

3x Kill Rule

Pause any ad set that spends 3x the target CPL with zero leads.

Spend > $105-$150 & Leads = 0 → PAUSE

Review creative, targeting, and form before restarting.

20% Scale Rule

If CPL is 20%+ below target for 5+ days, increase budget by 20%.

CPL < $28 for 5 days → +20% budget

Wait 3-5 days between increases. Never more than 20% at once.

Creative Refresh

Swap underperforming creatives every 2-3 weeks.

Frequency > 3.0 → Replace all creatives

Always have 2-3 backup creatives ready to deploy.

Consolidation

After Week 3, shift budget to top performers.

Winner gets 40-50% of total budget

Keep minimum 2 campaigns running for audience diversity.
